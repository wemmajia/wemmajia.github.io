Internship and Job Hunt Resources

Leetcode

https://leetcode.com/problemset/all/


There are over 2000 coding practice questions on leetcode and you need 100-200 problems under your belt with a good mix of easy, medium, and hard to be solid for a coding interview. Start leetcoding early! Aim to study at least the summer before your internship hunt.


Cracking the Coding Interview

https://www.amazon.com/Cracking-Coding-Interview-Programming-Questions/dp/0984782850


This book selects questions from sources such as leetcode and compiles what they believe to be the most important problems to do to prepare for the coding interview.


Handshake

https://careers.unc.edu/students/resources/handshake


Handshake offers a easy way to apply for internships and allows you to view UNC specific events such as career fairs that happen on campus.
